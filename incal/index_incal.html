FAQ常见问题

Does InCal upload my screenshots or text?InCal 会上传我的截图或文字吗？

No. OCR and schedule parsing happen entirely on your device. The app has no backend server and never sends your content to the network.不会。文字识别（OCR）和日程解析全部在你的设备上完成，App 没有后台服务器，也不会把内容发送到网络。

How do I turn a screenshot or text into an event?怎么把截图或文字变成日程？

On the home screen, paste text or import an image / scan a printed notice. InCal detects the date, time, location, and reminder and shows event cards. After reviewing, tap “Add” to save to Calendar, or export an .ics file.在首页粘贴文字，或点击导入图片 / 扫描纸质通知，InCal 会识别出日期、时间、地点和提醒并生成事件卡片；核对无误后点击「加入」写入系统日历，或导出 .ics 文件。

What if the time or title is wrong?识别的时间或标题不准怎么办？

Before saving, tap “Edit” on any event card to fix the time, location, title, or reminder. Results are a starting point — always review key details before saving. If a certain kind of text is consistently misread, email us a sample so we can improve it.在写入日历前，每张事件卡片都可以点击「编辑」修改时间、地点、标题和提醒。识别结果仅供参考，保存前请务必核对关键信息。若某类文字经常识别不准，欢迎把样本发到支持邮箱帮助我们改进。

Why did one message produce several events?为什么一段文字识别出了多个事件？

When a message contains several dates or times, InCal may split it into multiple candidate events (up to 3). If an extra event appears, just delete that card and keep the rest.当一段文字里包含多处日期/时间时，InCal 会尝试拆分成多个候选事件（最多 3 个）。如果出现多余的事件，直接在对应卡片上点击删除即可，其余保留。

What is “Scan Clipboard for Events”?「扫描剪贴板日程」是什么？

It's an optional, off-by-default feature. When enabled, InCal checks the clipboard for a date each time you open or return to it and asks before adding — handy right after you copy something. The check runs on device, nothing is uploaded, and you can turn it off in Settings anytime.这是一个默认关闭的可选功能。开启后，每次打开或切回 InCal 时会检查剪贴板里是否含有日期，并先询问再添加，便于你复制一段文字后快速建日程。检查在本机完成，内容不会上传，可随时在设置中关闭。

If I delete the app, do my saved events disappear?删除 App 后，已加入的日历事件会消失吗？

No. Events saved to the system calendar are managed by the Calendar app and are not removed when you delete InCal. InCal's local history is cleared when you uninstall.不会。写入系统日历的事件由「日历」App 管理，删除 InCal 不会移除它们。InCal 本地的历史记录则会随卸载一并清除。

Which iOS versions are supported?支持哪些系统版本？

InCal requires iOS 17 or later and supports Chinese and English.InCal 需要 iOS 17 或更高版本，支持中文与英文。

Privacy Policy隐私政策

Last updated: June 12, 2026最后更新：2026年6月12日

Overview概述

InCal is a local-first schedule recognition tool. Your screenshots, photos, and text are processed on your device by default and are not uploaded to a server. This policy explains how we handle information related to you.InCal 是一款本地优先的日程识别工具。我们的核心原则是：你的截图、照片和文字默认只在你的设备上处理，不会上传到任何服务器。本隐私政策说明我们如何处理与你相关的信息。

On-device processing本地处理

Image OCR and schedule parsing happen locally on your device. InCal does not include a backend server and does not send your images or text content to the network.图片的文字识别（OCR）、日程解析等全部在你的设备本地完成。InCal 不包含任何后台服务器，也不会将你的图片或文字内容发送到网络。

Clipboard剪贴板

Only when you turn on “Scan Clipboard for Events” in Settings does InCal check the clipboard for dates when you open or return to the app. The check runs on device, you are always asked before anything is added, and content is never uploaded. This feature is off by default and can be disabled at any time in Settings.仅当你在设置中开启「扫描剪贴板日程」后，InCal 才会在打开或切回 App 时检查剪贴板里是否含有日期。该检查在本机完成，识别到日程会先征得你同意再添加，内容不会上传。此功能默认关闭，可随时在设置中关闭。

Permissions we request我们申请的权限

To provide its features, InCal may request these permissions: • Photos: reads only the images you choose for recognition. The app does not scan your whole library. • Camera: used to scan printed notices or schedules only when you start scanning. • Calendar: used to save confirmed events to the system calendar. You can disable these permissions in Settings at any time. Related features will stop working when disabled.为提供功能，InCal 可能向你申请以下权限： • 照片：仅读取你主动选择的图片用于识别，App 不会扫描你的整个相册。 • 相机：用于「扫描」纸质通知或排班表，仅在你主动拍摄时使用。 • 日历：用于将你确认后的事件写入系统日历。 你可以随时在系统「设置」中关闭这些权限，关闭后相关功能将不可用。

Storage and deletion信息的存储与删除

Recognition history is stored locally on your device for review and reuse. You can delete it from History at any time, and uninstalling the app removes locally stored app data. Events saved to the system calendar are managed by Calendar, so deleting the app will not remove saved calendar events.识别历史记录保存在你的设备本地，便于你回看与再次使用。你可以随时在「历史记录」中删除这些内容，卸载 App 也会一并清除本地保存的数据。写入系统日历的事件由系统日历管理，删除 App 不会移除已写入的日历事件。

What we do not do我们不会做的事

• We do not upload, sell, or share your images or text content. • We do not require an account or collect your name, phone number, or email. • We do not integrate third-party advertising or behavioral tracking SDKs.• 不上传、不出售、不分享你的图片与文字内容。 • 不要求注册账号，不收集你的姓名、手机号或邮箱。 • 不集成第三方广告或行为追踪 SDK。

Third-party services第三方服务

InCal uses local Apple system capabilities, such as Vision text recognition and Calendar, to provide its features. These processes also happen on your device. We do not share your content with third parties for advertising or analytics.InCal 使用 Apple 系统自带的本地能力（如 Vision 文字识别、系统日历）完成功能，这些处理同样在你的设备上进行。我们不会为了广告或分析目的与第三方共享你的内容。
