隐私政策Privacy Policy

最后更新：2026年6月12日Last updated: June 12, 2026

概述Overview

Novacal 是一款本地优先的日程识别工具。我们的核心原则是：你的截图、照片和文字默认只在你的设备上处理，不会上传到任何服务器。本隐私政策说明我们如何处理与你相关的信息。Novacal is a local-first schedule recognition tool. Your screenshots, photos, and text are processed on your device by default and are not uploaded to a server. This policy explains how we handle information related to you.

本地处理On-device processing

图片的文字识别（OCR）、日程解析等全部在你的设备本地完成。Novacal 不包含任何后台服务器，也不会将你的图片或文字内容发送到网络。Image OCR and schedule parsing happen locally on your device. Novacal does not include a backend server and does not send your images or text content to the network.

剪贴板Clipboard

仅当你在设置中开启「扫描剪贴板日程」后，Novacal 才会在打开或切回 App 时检查剪贴板里是否含有日期。该检查在本机完成，识别到日程会先征得你同意再添加，内容不会上传。此功能默认关闭，可随时在设置中关闭。Only when you turn on “Scan Clipboard for Events” in Settings does Novacal check the clipboard for dates when you open or return to the app. The check runs on device, you are always asked before anything is added, and content is never uploaded. This feature is off by default and can be disabled at any time in Settings.

我们申请的权限Permissions we request

为提供功能，Novacal 可能向你申请以下权限： • 照片：仅读取你主动选择的图片用于识别，App 不会扫描你的整个相册。 • 相机：用于「扫描」纸质通知或排班表，仅在你主动拍摄时使用。 • 日历：用于将你确认后的事件写入系统日历。 你可以随时在系统「设置」中关闭这些权限，关闭后相关功能将不可用。To provide its features, Novacal may request these permissions: • Photos: reads only the images you choose for recognition. The app does not scan your whole library. • Camera: used to scan printed notices or schedules only when you start scanning. • Calendar: used to save confirmed events to the system calendar. You can disable these permissions in Settings at any time. Related features will stop working when disabled.

信息的存储与删除Storage and deletion

识别历史记录保存在你的设备本地，便于你回看与再次使用。你可以随时在「历史记录」中删除这些内容，卸载 App 也会一并清除本地保存的数据。写入系统日历的事件由系统日历管理，删除 App 不会移除已写入的日历事件。Recognition history is stored locally on your device for review and reuse. You can delete it from History at any time, and uninstalling the app removes locally stored app data. Events saved to the system calendar are managed by Calendar, so deleting the app will not remove saved calendar events.

我们不会做的事What we do not do

• 不上传、不出售、不分享你的图片与文字内容。 • 不要求注册账号，不收集你的姓名、手机号或邮箱。 • 不集成第三方广告或行为追踪 SDK。• We do not upload, sell, or share your images or text content. • We do not require an account or collect your name, phone number, or email. • We do not integrate third-party advertising or behavioral tracking SDKs.

第三方服务Third-party services

Novacal 使用 Apple 系统自带的本地能力（如 Vision 文字识别、系统日历）完成功能，这些处理同样在你的设备上进行。我们不会为了广告或分析目的与第三方共享你的内容。Novacal uses local Apple system capabilities, such as Vision text recognition and Calendar, to provide its features. These processes also happen on your device. We do not share your content with third parties for advertising or analytics.

儿童隐私Children's privacy

Novacal 不面向 13 周岁以下儿童，也不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。Novacal is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect children's personal information.

政策更新Policy updates

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。发生重大变更时，会在 App 内提示。继续使用即表示你接受更新后的政策。We may update this privacy policy from time to time. For material changes, we will provide notice in the app. Continued use means you accept the updated policy.
