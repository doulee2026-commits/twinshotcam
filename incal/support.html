常见问题FAQ

Novacal 会上传我的截图或文字吗？Does Novacal upload my screenshots or text?

不会。文字识别（OCR）和日程解析全部在你的设备上完成，App 没有后台服务器，也不会把内容发送到网络。No. OCR and schedule parsing happen entirely on your device. The app has no backend server and never sends your content to the network.

怎么把截图或文字变成日程？How do I turn a screenshot or text into an event?

在首页粘贴文字，或点击导入图片 / 扫描纸质通知，Novacal 会识别出日期、时间、地点和提醒并生成事件卡片；核对无误后点击「加入」写入系统日历，或导出 .ics 文件。On the home screen, paste text or import an image / scan a printed notice. Novacal detects the date, time, location, and reminder and shows event cards. After reviewing, tap “Add” to save to Calendar, or export an .ics file.

识别的时间或标题不准怎么办？What if the time or title is wrong?

在写入日历前，每张事件卡片都可以点击「编辑」修改时间、地点、标题和提醒。识别结果仅供参考，保存前请务必核对关键信息。若某类文字经常识别不准，欢迎把样本发到支持邮箱帮助我们改进。Before saving, tap “Edit” on any event card to fix the time, location, title, or reminder. Results are a starting point — always review key details before saving. If a certain kind of text is consistently misread, email us a sample so we can improve it.

为什么一段文字识别出了多个事件？Why did one message produce several events?

当一段文字里包含多处日期/时间时，Novacal 会尝试拆分成多个候选事件（最多 3 个）。如果出现多余的事件，直接在对应卡片上点击删除即可，其余保留。When a message contains several dates or times, Novacal may split it into multiple candidate events (up to 3). If an extra event appears, just delete that card and keep the rest.

「扫描剪贴板日程」是什么？What is “Scan Clipboard for Events”?

这是一个默认关闭的可选功能。开启后，每次打开或切回 Novacal 时会检查剪贴板里是否含有日期，并先询问再添加，便于你复制一段文字后快速建日程。检查在本机完成，内容不会上传，可随时在设置中关闭。It's an optional, off-by-default feature. When enabled, Novacal checks the clipboard for a date each time you open or return to it and asks before adding — handy right after you copy something. The check runs on device, nothing is uploaded, and you can turn it off in Settings anytime.

删除 App 后，已加入的日历事件会消失吗？If I delete the app, do my saved events disappear?

不会。写入系统日历的事件由「日历」App 管理，删除 Novacal 不会移除它们。Novacal 本地的历史记录则会随卸载一并清除。No. Events saved to the system calendar are managed by the Calendar app and are not removed when you delete Novacal. Novacal's local history is cleared when you uninstall.

支持哪些系统版本？Which iOS versions are supported?

Novacal 需要 iOS 17 或更高版本，支持中文与英文。Novacal requires iOS 17 or later and supports Chinese and English.
