隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

1. 总览

TwinShotCam（“戏精相机”）是一款完全在你的设备本机运行的相机应用。我们的设计原则是：你的内容属于你，我们不需要、也不会收集它。

2. 我们收集什么

答案是：什么都不收集。我们没有账号系统、没有服务器、没有任何分析、追踪、推送或广告 SDK，也不申请任何网络访问例外。

3. 相机与麦克风

应用使用相机进行前后双摄录制，使用麦克风录制视频声音。所有画面与声音仅在你的设备本机实时处理与合成，生成的视频只保存在你的系统相册中，由你完全掌控。我们不会上传、存储或访问这些内容。

4. 面部数据说明

“不要笑挑战”使用设备的 ARKit / Vision 能力，实时判断你是否在微笑或大笑。该处理仅在本机实时进行，用于驱动游戏逻辑。我们不单独保存任何面部特征数据，更不会上传或与任何第三方共享。

5. 相册访问

应用在你保存视频时请求相册写入权限，在你导入自定义挑战素材时请求读取权限。访问仅用于你主动发起的操作，不会扫描或读取你相册中的其他内容。

6. 内购与订阅

Pro 订阅与买断通过 Apple 的 App Store 内购（StoreKit）完成。支付由 Apple 处理，我们不接触你的支付信息。订阅管理与取消可在你的 App Store 账户设置中进行。

7. 第三方 SDK

无。应用不集成任何第三方分析、追踪、推送或广告组件。

8. 儿童隐私

由于本应用不收集任何个人数据，因此不会收集儿童的个人信息。

9. 你的权利

你随时可以删除应用以清除所有本地数据。由于我们不持有你的任何数据，无需向我们提出删除请求。

10. 政策更新

若本政策有任何变更，我们会更新本页面并修改下方的“最后更新”日期。

11. 联系我们

如有任何疑问，请联系 doulee2026@hotmail.com。

1. Overview

TwinShotCam is a camera app that runs entirely on your device. Our design principle is simple: your content belongs to you. We don't need it, and we don't collect it.

2. What We Collect

The answer is: nothing at all. There are no accounts, no servers, and no analytics, tracking, push, or advertising SDKs. We request no network access exceptions.

3. Camera & Microphone

The app uses the camera for front-and-back dual recording, and the microphone to capture audio. All video and audio are processed and composited locally on your device in real time. The resulting videos are saved only to your system Photos library, fully under your control. We never upload, store, or access this content.

4. Facial Data

The "Hold Your Laugh" challenge uses on-device ARKit / Vision to detect in real time whether you are smiling or laughing. This processing happens entirely on-device to drive the game. We do not store any facial feature data separately, nor do we upload or share it with any third party.

5. Photo Library Access

The app requests write access to save videos, and read access when you import your own challenge clips. Access is used solely for actions you initiate; we do not scan or read other content in your library.

6. In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

Pro subscriptions and the lifetime unlock are handled through Apple's App Store In-App Purchase (StoreKit). Payments are processed by Apple — we never see your payment information. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your App Store account settings.

7. Third-Party SDKs

None. The app integrates no third-party analytics, tracking, push, or advertising components.

8. Children's Privacy

Because the app collects no personal data, it does not collect personal information from children.

9. Your Rights

You can delete the app at any time to erase all local data. Since we hold none of your data, no deletion request to us is necessary.

10. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date below.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions, contact doulee2026@hotmail.com.
