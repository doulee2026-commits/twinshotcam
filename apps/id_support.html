Frequently asked questions

Where are my photos processed?

Everything runs locally using Apple's Vision framework. There is no server and no account — your images are never uploaded.

How do I remove the watermark?

Free export adds a watermark. A one-time in-app purchase (Pro Unlock) permanently removes it and exports full-quality images. No subscription, no auto-renewal.

I bought Pro but it's locked on a new phone.

Open the app's Settings page and tap “Restore Purchases”. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the purchase.

How do I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in, find SnapID Studio, and request a refund.

Which photo sizes are supported?

39 specifications across 6 categories — standard ID sizes, language tests (IELTS/TOEFL/GRE/PTE), students, travel visas, professional licenses, and government use.

The compliance check keeps warning me.

It only blocks when no face / multiple faces are found, or the head is clearly turned or tilted. Other notes (background, framing) are advisory and never stop you from continuing.

Will my photo pass an official review?

Requirements vary by document, visa, and exam. SnapID helps you match common specs, but acceptance is decided by the receiving authority.

常见问题

我的照片在哪里处理？

全部基于 Apple Vision 框架在本机完成，无服务器、无需账号，照片不会上传。

如何去除水印？

免费导出带水印。一次性内购（Pro 解锁）可永久去除水印并导出高清原图，无订阅、无自动续费。

买了 Pro，换新手机后又被锁了。

进入 App 的设置页，点击「恢复购买」。请确认登录的是当初购买所用的同一个 Apple ID。

如何申请退款？

退款由 Apple 处理。访问 reportaproblem.apple.com，登录后找到 SnapID Studio 申请退款。

支持哪些证件照尺寸？

覆盖 6 大分类、39 种规格——寸照、语言考试（雅思/托福/GRE/PTE）、学生、出国签证、职业资格、社保政务。

合规检查老是提示我。

只有「未检测到人脸 / 画面多张脸 / 头部明显侧转或倾斜」才会拦截；背景、构图等只是提示，不会阻止你继续。

生成的照片能通过官方审核吗？

不同证件、签证、考试要求各异。本应用帮你匹配常用规格，但是否通过由受理机构决定。

SnapID Studio Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 20, 2026

SnapID Studio (the “App”) respects and protects your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles information related to you. Please read it carefully before use.

1. Core principle: on-device processing

All image processing in the App (portrait cutout, auto-framing, background replacement, beautify, outfit, and 6-inch layout) runs entirely on your device using Apple's built-in Vision framework. The App has no server, requires no account or sign-in, and your photos and image data are never uploaded to any server.

2. Information we do not collect

The App does not collect, store, or share: photo or image content; personal identifiers such as name, phone number, or ID number; device identifiers; location; contacts; or usage/analytics data.

3. Permissions

  • Camera: used only when you actively take a photo, to capture an ID photo.
  • Photo library (add): used only when you confirm export, to save the generated ID photo to your album.
  • Choosing a photo: done through the system photo picker; the App receives only the single photo you select and cannot access your entire library.

You can review and manage these permissions anytime in Settings › Privacy & Security.

4. Local storage

Temporary data created while making a photo is stored only on your device to complete the current task; exported files are managed by the system once you save them to your album.

5. Third-party services

The App integrates no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The only system services involved are the App Store (rating, sharing, and in-app purchases are provided by Apple and governed by Apple's privacy policy).

5.1 In-app purchases

The App offers a one-time “Pro Unlock” in-app purchase (to remove the export watermark). Purchase, payment, and restore are handled entirely by the Apple App Store; the App does not collect or store your payment information. Unlock status is verified on-device from the system purchase receipt and nothing is uploaded.

6. Children's privacy

The App is intended for general audiences, is not directed to children under 13, and does not knowingly collect children's personal information.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced through in-app release notes or on this page; continued use means you accept the updated policy.

8. Contact us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact: doulee2026@hotmail.com

SnapID Studio 隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 6 月 20 日

SnapID Studio（以下简称“本应用”）尊重并保护你的隐私。本政策说明本应用如何处理与你相关的信息，请在使用前仔细阅读。

1. 核心原则：本机处理

本应用的全部图像处理（人像分割抠图、自动取景、更换底色、美颜、换装、六寸排版）均在你的设备本地完成，使用 Apple 系统内置的 Vision 框架。本应用不设服务器，无需注册或登录账号，你的照片与图像数据不会被上传到任何服务器。

2. 我们不收集的信息

本应用不收集、不存储、不共享：照片或图像内容；姓名、手机号、证件号等个人身份信息；设备标识符；地理位置；通讯录；使用行为或统计分析数据。

3. 权限说明

  • 相机：仅在你主动拍摄时使用，用于现场拍摄证件照。
  • 相册（添加）：仅在你确认导出时，用于把生成的证件照保存到你的相册。
  • 选择照片：通过系统相册选择器进行，本应用仅获得你所选的那张照片，无法访问你的整个相册。

你可随时在“系统设置 > 隐私与安全性”中查看与管理上述权限。

4. 本地存储

制作过程中产生的临时数据仅保存在你的设备本地，用于完成当前制作；导出文件在你保存到相册后由系统管理。

5. 第三方服务

本应用不集成任何第三方统计、广告或追踪 SDK。涉及的系统服务仅限 App Store（评分、分享与应用内购买由 Apple 提供，并受 Apple 隐私政策约束）。

5.1 应用内购买

本应用提供一次性「Pro 解锁」内购（用于去除导出水印）。购买、支付与恢复均由 Apple App Store 处理，本应用不收集、不存储你的支付信息；解锁状态由系统的购买凭证在本机校验，不上传任何数据。

6. 儿童隐私

本应用面向一般用户，不针对 13 岁以下儿童，也不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。

7. 政策更新

我们可能不时更新本政策。重大变更将通过应用内更新说明或本页面公示，继续使用即表示你接受更新后的政策。

8. 联系我们
